About Me

He received his PhD degree from the Department of Computer Science and Technology, Nanjing University in 2023, under the supervision of Professor Wu-Jun Li. Prior to that, he received his B.Sc. degree from the Department of Computer Science and Technology, Nanjing University in 2016. See CV for a brief introduction about Kai-Lang Yao.
